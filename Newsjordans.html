Jordan Brand Unveils Holiday 2023 Retro Footwear Releases

Jordan Brand has officially revealed its Holiday 2023 Retro Footwear line-up, covering the majority of the drops scheduled for the months of October, November, and December to close out the year.

This preview obviously does not encompass all that Jordan Brand has to offer this season as there are likely to be some kids-exclusive offerings as well as collaborations on the release slate, but in terms of widely available product with high distribution numbers, this covers the full gamut.

Starting off is the Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG in three striking styles — the women’s exclusive “Satin Bred”, the suede-clad “Royal Reimagined”, and the attractive “Sky J Mauve”. The Air Jordan 11, a seasonal mainstay, appears in the DMP-inspired “Gratitude” and the women’s exclusive “Neapolitan”. Arguably the most exciting of the bunch is the return of the original white/red Air Jordan 12 also known as the “Cherry”.

There are some other key releases scattered within, like the return of the Air Jordan 3 “Fear”, another Air Jordan 4 SE Craft, and an Air Jordan 2 inspired by the silhouette’s Italian roots.
