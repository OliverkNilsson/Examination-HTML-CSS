First Look At The Air Jordan 8 Winter “Gunsmoke”

With the original “Playoffs” colorway officially returning at the end of September and SoleFly already preparing a collaborative release, the Air Jordan 8 could be the next “it” Retro to pace Jordan Brand’s footwear offering for the next few seasons. Luckily, you won’t have to wait much longer for the next AJ8 to hit stores, because a season-ready iteration will be ready at the end of the November.

This Air Jordan 8 Winter comes equipped with beefed up materials designed to battle the colder, inclement weather. Featuring a full black upper, Metallic Silver accents on the midsole and ankle straps, and Gunsmoke hits on the mudguard and outsole, this Air Jordan 8 is less an in-game shoe and more a cold climate style option as it made specifically to battle those harsher conditions as it features a thicker lining on the interior.

Jordan Brand has just revealed a first look as part of its full Holiday 2023 preview, expected a release on November 27th, 2023 for $210.
